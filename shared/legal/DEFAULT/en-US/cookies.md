## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. **winegod.ai** uses a minimal set of cookies and local storage to make the product work.

## 2. Cookies we use

- **`wg_locale_choice`** — the language you manually selected. Valid for 1 year.
- **`wg_locale_choice_at`** — a timestamp of when the preference was set, used to detect changes.
- **Vercel technical cookies** — set by the hosting platform to serve the application.

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

## 3. Local storage

We use `localStorage` and `sessionStorage` to keep the session alive and to store the ongoing chat history. That data stays on your device and can be cleared via your browser settings.

## 4. How to manage

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. If you block the language cookie, winegod.ai still works, but you will need to pick the language again each session.
