## Your data, your call

You have the right to request the deletion of the personal data associated with your account at **winegod.ai**.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your profile data (name, email, profile picture)
- The link to your sign-in provider (Google, Facebook, Apple, or Microsoft)
- Your full chat history with Baco
- Every saved conversation (favorites)

## What does not get deleted

- Anonymized usage records (unlinked from your account) that we keep for aggregated product analytics
- Data stored locally in your browser — cleared automatically on account deletion, but you can also wipe it manually in your browser settings

## Data held by third parties

Deleting your account at winegod.ai removes your data from our systems. To revoke winegod.ai’s access inside your sign-in provider, use the provider’s own security settings:

- **Google:** Account settings > Security > Third-party apps
- **Facebook:** Settings > Apps and websites
- **Apple:** Settings > Apple ID > Apps using Apple ID
- **Microsoft:** Account > Privacy > Apps and services

## Questions

For any question about data deletion or privacy, reach out at [privacy@winegod.ai](mailto:privacy@winegod.ai).
