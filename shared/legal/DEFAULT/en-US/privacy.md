## 1. Who we are

**winegod.ai** is an AI service focused on wine, accessible at [chat.winegod.ai](https://chat.winegod.ai). This document describes how we handle data from the people who use the product.

## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 Account data (OAuth sign-in)

When you sign in with Google, Facebook, Apple, or Microsoft, we receive from the chosen provider: name, email, and profile picture. We never receive your password.

### 2.2 Usage data

- Messages you send in the chat (text, photos, videos, PDFs)
- Record of credits consumed (amount, type, date)
- Session identifier and IP address

### 2.3 Local storage

We use **localStorage** and **sessionStorage** in your browser to keep the session alive and to display the current session’s chat history. That data stays on your device.

## 3. How we use your data

- Provide personalized wine answers
- Track message credits (daily cap)
- Improve the quality of the service

**We do not sell your personal data.**

## 4. Third-party services

To function, winegod.ai relies on external services:

- **Anthropic (Claude)** — natural language processing for the chat
- **Google (Gemini)** — visual reading of photos and PDFs (OCR)
- **Google, Facebook, Apple, Microsoft** — OAuth sign-in
- **Render** — backend and database hosting
- **Vercel** — frontend hosting

Each provider has its own privacy policy. Chat messages are processed by Anthropic; for photos and PDFs, also by Google Gemini.

## 5. Data retention

Your account data stays while your account exists. Usage records (credits consumed) are kept for internal control. You can request deletion of your data as described at [Data deletion](/legal/DEFAULT/en-US/data-deletion).

## 6. Your rights

You can:

- Request access to your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise those rights, contact us at [privacy@winegod.ai](mailto:privacy@winegod.ai).
